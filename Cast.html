James Roday was the first actor cast for Psych! He is a master improvist and dated Maggie Lawson for most of the show's running.

Dule Hill, Burton Guster, was the most accomplished actor on set at the time, having starred in The West Wing. He and James initially clashed but became close friends.

Maggie Lawson, starring as Juliet O'Hara, is a talented actress who has appeared in Hallmark films and other TV shows. She is also a huge animal lover.

Timothy Omundson, who plays Carlton Lassiter, is a veteran actor with Broadway and TV roles. He survived a major stroke in 2018 and continues to recover and thrive.
