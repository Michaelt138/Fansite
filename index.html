Cast Characters

Originally Airing in 2006 Psych ran for a total of eight seasons! Psych is a comedy series following Shawn Spencer, a (fake) psych detective for the Santa Barbara Police Department. His childhood best friend Burton Guster, a pharmaceutical salesman and the other half of the Psych detective agency!

Psych has been off of the air since 2014 but since then they have produced three movies and the cast and crew are all still very close. Maggie Lawson and Timothy Omundson have created a podcast named "The Psych-ologists are in" where they rewatch and retell stories about filming!
