Origionally Airing in 2006 Psych ran for a total of eight seasons and three movies! Psych is a comedy series following Shawn Spencer, a (fake) psych detective for the Santa Barbra Police Department. His childhood best friend Burton Guster, a pharmaceutical salesmen and the other half of the Psych detective agency!

Psych was filmed in Vancouver B.C. with many shots taking place on the pier in White Rock. For members of the PNW it is a short trip to view filming locations!
